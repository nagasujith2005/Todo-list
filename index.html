    Quick Help

    Adding Tasks

    Type your task in the input field, select category and priority, then click "Add Task" or press Enter.

    Searching

    Use the search bar to find tasks by text or category. Results update in real-time.

    Filtering

    Use the filter buttons and dropdowns to view specific tasks by status, category, or priority.

    Completing Tasks

    Click the circle next to any task to mark it as completed. Completed tasks show with a strikethrough.

    Privacy Policy

    Data Collection

    TaskMaster Pro stores your task data locally in your browser. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to our servers.

    Local Storage

    Your tasks, settings, and preferences are stored in your browser's localStorage. This data remains on your device and is not shared with us.

    Third-Party Services

    We use Font Awesome for icons and Google Fonts for typography. These services may collect usage analytics.

    Data Export

    You can export your data at any time using the settings panel. This creates a JSON file containing all your tasks and settings.

    Contact

    If you have questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at privacy@taskmasterpro.com
